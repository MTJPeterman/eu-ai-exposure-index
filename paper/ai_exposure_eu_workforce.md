# AI Exposure in the European Workforce

## Research question

Can we build a reproducible Europe-wide AI exposure index using official labour market data and an explicit task-level scoring method?

## Data

- Eurostat employment by occupation
- Eurostat earnings by occupation
- ESCO occupations, skills, and knowledge items
- LLM scoring rubric for task exposure

## Why this matters

Most AI job-exposure charts online collapse jobs into a single score without clean task logic, and many mix incompatible datasets. This repository tries to fix that by making the mapping, scoring, and aggregation steps explicit.

## Core contribution

A reproducible EU index that separates:

- technical exposure
- wage exposure
- likely task compression

## Limitation to state clearly

This index measures technical feasibility, not legal adoption, organisational inertia, or realised job loss.
